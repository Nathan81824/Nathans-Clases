Common Questions

How long does a typical project take?

A basic website takes 1–2 weeks. A full web app with custom features typically takes 4–8 weeks depending on complexity. I'll give you a clear timeline after our first call.

Do you work with clients outside Nigeria?

Absolutely. I work with clients globally and am comfortable with remote collaboration using tools like Slack, Notion, Figma, and Zoom.

Do you offer revisions?

Yes — every project includes 2 rounds of revisions at no extra cost. I work closely with clients throughout to minimise the need for major changes at the end.

What do you need from me to get started?

Just reach out via the form above with a brief description of what you need. I'll ask for more specifics (branding, content, reference sites) after we agree to work together.
